School Cake

The Cake:

A delicious and simple vanilla school cake with icing and sprinkles – the classic easy bake for everyone!

Ingredients

  • 280 g Margarine
  • 5 Free-range Eggs
  • 280 g Caster Sugar
  • 280 g Self-raising Flour
  • 300 g Icing Sugar
  • Few teaspoons of boiling water
  • Sprinkles to decorate

Method

  1. Pre-heat your oven to 180°c/Fan 160°c/Gas Mark 4 and line a tin with greaseproof paper.

  2. In a large bowl, cream together the margarine and sugar until light and fluffy.

  3. Add the eggs one at time, ensuring each is mixed through before adding the next.

  4. Sift in the flour and gently fold to create a batter.

  5. Transfer the batter to your pre-lined tin and bake in your pre-heated oven for 50-60 minutes until risen, golden and a skewer inserted comes out clean.

  6. Leave to cool in the tin for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.

  7. When your cake has cooled completely; in a bowl, mix the icing sugar with a few teaspoons of boiling water, adding a little water at time. Pour the icing onto your cake and Finish with some rainbow sprinkles.
